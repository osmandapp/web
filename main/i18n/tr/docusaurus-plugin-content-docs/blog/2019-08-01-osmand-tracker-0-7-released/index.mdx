### Live track

<p>Now you can see the online moving of your contacts on the map of OsmAnd with online GPS-track. For this, you should switch on "Live" at your contact in "Timeline menu". If there are many sharing contacts at this moment OsmAnd app chooses the color of tracks by random.</p>
