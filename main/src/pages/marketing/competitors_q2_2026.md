## Key Competitive Trends — Q2 2026

### 1. Car and wearable expansion

**Locus Map**
- Launched Android Auto.

**Guru Maps**
- Launched Apple Watch support.

This increases direct competition with OsmAnd's multi-platform navigation strategy:

- Smartphone
- Android Auto / CarPlay
- Wearables
- Web

---

### 2. Organic Maps is rapidly expanding its feature set

Organic Maps added:

- Satellite imagery
- Public transport routing
- Alternate routes
- Elevation charts
- Improved route planning

This moves Organic Maps further away from being simply a lightweight offline OSM viewer/navigation app.

---

### 3. Route difficulty and elevation are becoming more important

**Komoot**
- Automatic Climb Detection.

**Mapy.com**
- Difficult-section warnings.

**Organic Maps**
- Elevation charts.

**Locus Map**
- Improved track charts.

Competitors are increasingly helping users understand route characteristics before and during navigation, not merely displaying the route.

---

### 4. Live and social navigation is growing

**onX Offroad**
- Real-time group Location Sharing.

**Strava**
- More hiking discovery, navigation, and social functionality.

**Outdooractive**
- Challenges and brand partnerships.

Navigation products are increasingly becoming community ecosystems rather than only utilities.

---

### 5. Hybrid online + offline mapping is strengthening

**Guru Maps**
- Streaming vector/elevation maps with local caching.

**CoMaps**
- Map updates independent from application releases.

**Organic Maps**
- Frequent downloadable OSM map updates.

Competitors are increasingly combining:

- Offline reliability
- Faster online map delivery
- More frequent data updates

---

### 6. AI is moving into route and place discovery

**Apple Maps**
- AI-enhanced Flyover.
- Local Lists.

**Wikiloc**
- Conversational outdoor-route discovery through ChatGPT.

However, the most important Q2 innovation in offline/outdoor navigation remained concentrated around:

- Android Auto
- Public transport
- Elevation and climb information
- Route alternatives
- Live location sharing
- Map delivery

# Main Competitors to Watch After Q2 2026

## Locus Map

**Why:**
- Android Auto puts Locus Map into more direct competition with OsmAnd.
- Continues expanding advanced outdoor and navigation functionality.

## Organic Maps

**Why:**
- Very rapid expansion of functionality.
- Satellite imagery.
- Public transport routing.
- Alternate routes.
- Elevation charts.
- Still free and open-source.

## Mapy.com

**Why:**
- Strong outdoor UX.
- Route difficulty information.
- Strong route discovery and planning.
- Offline ecosystem continues improving.

## Komoot

**Why:**
- Strong improvements to active navigation.
- Automatic Climb Detection.
- Route Planner improvements.
- Strong community ecosystem.

## Guru Maps

**Why:**
- Hybrid streamed/offline vector maps.
- Apple Watch support.
- Existing Android Auto support.
- Increasingly broad cross-platform navigation ecosystem.

## onX Offroad

**Why:**
- Strong differentiation in the U.S. off-road market.
- Real-time group location sharing creates a strong safety/community feature.
